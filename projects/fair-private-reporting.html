Purpose of the Study

This study aims to design a more effective reporting system that respects individual privacy and ensures due process.

Study Procedures

In the one-hour interview, participants will share their experiences with the reporting system within online communities,
as well as their opinions on various proposed designs for reporting systems.

Benefits

You may learn more about reporting systems in online communities and get an opportunity to inform the design of future reporting mechanisms with their own testimonies.

Risks

We foresee extremely minimal risks with this study.
While harassment scenarios may include offensive languages, we design intricate study procedures to prevent any unnecessary exposure and harms.

Protecting Your Information

The interview can be conducted using a video conferencing tool or audio call.
If participants choose video or audio chats, the interview will be recorded but for internal purposes only. We will also keep your identity anonymous.

Compensation

If selected participants are willing to share their email address and name, they will receive a $20 Amazon gift card.
