I am an undergraduate student at Tsinghua University and major in Computer Science. Currently, I am working as a research assistant at Social AI Group at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am co-advised by Professors Haiyi Zhu and Steven Wu. I am also fortunate to be part of CMU DIG lab and advised by Professor Adam Perer.

My current research interests are Social Computing, Fairness, Accountability, and Transparency, and, more broadly, Human-AI Interaction. Specifically, I am passionate to (1) investigate the interaction between algorithms and humans and (2) design human-centered systems that reflect the human values and needs of real-world stakeholders.
